Relaydex Android Closed Test

Test the Android remote for local Codex on Windows.

Relaydex is an independent fork of Remodex focused on controlling local Codex running on your own Windows PC from Android. This is not the official Remodex app.

What the app does

  • Pair with your host machine using a QR code
  • Open and create Codex threads from Android
  • Send prompts to local Codex
  • Receive streaming output
  • Handle approval requests from your phone

The app does not run Codex on the phone itself. Codex still runs locally on your own computer.

How to join

  1. Join the tester group
  2. Opt in on Google Play
  3. Install the app from Google Play
  4. Keep the app installed and remain opted in for at least 14 days
Join the Google Group https://groups.google.com/g/relaydex-android-testers Opt in on Google Play https://play.google.com/apps/testing/io.relaydex.android Install the test app https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=io.relaydex.android
